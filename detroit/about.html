About Detroit Live Stream

Where laughter takes the mic — streaming Detroit’s best stand-up comedy live to the world.

Our Mission

Detroit Live Stream exists to spotlight the city’s comedy culture. We broadcast live stand-up shows, local open mics, and rising stars who bring humor and honesty to every stage.

We’re not just streaming laughs — we’re sharing Detroit’s voice, one punchline at a time.

What We Stream

Our lineup includes comedy nights, interviews, behind-the-scenes content, and spontaneous street shows. Every stream captures the raw, electric atmosphere that makes live performance unforgettable.

Join the Experience

Whether you’re watching from home or performing on stage, Detroit Live Stream connects you to the heart of the action. Follow us and be part of a community built on laughter, creativity, and connection.
